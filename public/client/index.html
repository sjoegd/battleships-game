Help Page

Ship Positioning:

  • To move a ship: left click on a ship and move the mouse
  • To rotate: press R while holding a ship

Finding a game:

  • This game is multiplayer, so you need to find another person to play it
  • To find such a person, click find game, which will put you in a game againt a person

Gameplay:

  • To attack: left click on a certain grid in the right matrix
  • A white dot, means you hit water
  • A black dot, means you hit a ship part
  • A ship part's ship is destroyed if red crosses appear over it

Winning:

  • The only way to win in this game is to destroy all the enemy ships
  • Or the enemy disconnects..
  • After winning or losing your game will automatically reset
