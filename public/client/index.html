Help Page

Ship Positioning:

  • Left click and hold on a ship to move it
  • While holding the ship, you can press "r" to rotate it

Finding a game:

  • To find a game with a random opponent, click the find game button, this will put you in a queue and put you in a game if an enemy is found

Gameplay:

  • Left click on a grid block in the right (enemy) matrix to attack
  • If you hit an attack, you can attack again till you either destroy a ship or miss
  • A white dot, means you hit water
  • A black dot, means you hit a ship part
  • A ship part's ship is destroyed if red crosses appear over it

Winning:

  • The only way to win in this game is to destroy all the enemy ships
  • Or the enemy disconnects..
  • After winning or losing your game will automatically reset, so that you can play again
